DESTINATION: Manali
STATE: Himachal Pradesh
TYPE: Hill Station, Adventure, Nature, Honeymoon
BEST_TIME: October to June (snow: Dec-Feb, flowers: Mar-May, adventure: Jun-Sep)
AVOID: July-August (heavy monsoon, landslides)
ALTITUDE: 2,050 m (Manali town), 3,978 m (Rohtang Pass)
HIGHLIGHTS:
- Rohtang Pass (3,978m): Snow point, skiing, snowboarding — open May to Nov
- Solang Valley: Paragliding, zorbing, skiing, snowmobiling
- Old Manali: Cafes, backpacker culture, Manu Temple, Hadimba Devi Temple
- Beas River: White water rafting Grade 3-4, best Jun-Aug
- Naggar Castle: 500-year-old heritage castle, Nicholas Roerich Art Gallery
- Kasol (90km): Parvati Valley, trekking, cannabis culture, Israeli cuisine
- Kheerganga Trek: 12km trek, natural hot spring at top
- Chandrakhani Pass Trek: 3-day moderate trek with Kullu valley views
- Hampta Pass Trek: 4-day high altitude crossover to Lahaul-Spiti
BUDGET_RANGE:
- Budget: ₹2,500-4,000/day (hostel, local dhabas, shared transport)
- Mid-range: ₹5,000-9,000/day (3-star hotel, restaurant meals, taxi)
- Luxury: ₹12,000-25,000/day (resort, adventure packages, private cab)
IDEAL_FOR: Couples, Adventure seekers, Solo backpackers, Groups, Families
DISTANCE_FROM_DELHI: 570 km (12-14 hrs by bus, 10-12 hrs by car)
NEAREST_AIRPORT: Bhuntar Airport (Kullu-Manali), 50 km from Manali
LANGUAGE: Hindi, Pahadi, English (tourist areas)
PERMITS_REQUIRED: Inner Line Permit for Rohtang Pass (₹500 online at rohtangpermits.nic.in)
FAMOUS_TREK: Hampta Pass, Chandrakhani, Deo Tibba, Beas Kund
FAMOUS_ACTIVITIES: Paragliding (₹1,500-2,500), River Rafting (₹600-1,200/person), Skiing (Dec-Mar)
CONNECTIVITY: BSNL/Airtel/Jio works in Manali town, poor beyond

DESTINATION: Leh-Ladakh
STATE: Ladakh (Union Territory)
TYPE: Adventure, Scenic, Spiritual, Motorcycle touring
BEST_TIME: June to September (roads open, weather clear)
AVOID: November to April (most roads closed, extreme cold -20°C to -30°C)
ALTITUDE: 3,524 m (Leh city) — acclimatization mandatory
HIGHLIGHTS:
- Pangong Tso Lake (4,350m): 134km long, changes 7 shades of blue — 5 hrs from Leh
- Nubra Valley: Khardung La (5,359m world's highest motorable road), Bactrian camel safari at Hunder
- Tso Moriri Lake (4,595m): Remote, serene, flamingos in summer
- Magnetic Hill: Optical illusion road near Leh
- Hall of Fame Museum: Indian Army tribute, recommended visit
- Shanti Stupa: White dome stupa, sunrise/sunset views over Leh
- Leh Palace: 17th century palace overlooking old city
- Hemis Monastery: Largest monastery in Ladakh, Hemis Festival (Jun-Jul)
- Thiksey Monastery: Mini Potala Palace on a hill
- Zanskar Valley: Remote, extreme terrain, frozen Chadar Trek (winter)
- Sham Valley Trek: Easy 3-day trek for beginners (Likir, Yangthang, Hemis Shukpachan)
BUDGET_RANGE:
- Budget: ₹3,000-5,000/day (guesthouse, local meals, shared jeep)
- Mid-range: ₹7,000-12,000/day (hotel, restaurant, private taxi)
- Luxury: ₹15,000-35,000/day (premium camps at Pangong, Nubra)
IDEAL_FOR: Adventure seekers, Bikers, Solo travelers, Photographers, Couples
NEAREST_AIRPORT: Kushok Bakula Rimpochhe Airport (LEH) — daily flights from Delhi, Mumbai
PERMITS:
- Inner Line Permit required for Pangong, Nubra, Tso Moriri, Dha-Hanu
- Obtain online at lahdc.nic.in or at DC Office Leh (₹200-400 per area)
- Protected Area Permit for foreigners (from DC Office Leh)
HEALTH: Acute Mountain Sickness (AMS) is real risk. Rest 24-48 hrs on arrival. Diamox on prescription. Carry oxygen.
CONNECTIVITY: Only BSNL postpaid works beyond Leh. Buy BSNL SIM at Leh Airport.
FAMOUS_ROUTES: Manali-Leh Highway (480km, 2 days), Srinagar-Leh Highway (434km, 2 days)
BIKE_TRIP: Royal Enfield preferred, hire from Leh at ₹1,200-2,000/day

DESTINATION: Rishikesh
STATE: Uttarakhand
TYPE: Spiritual, Adventure, Yoga, Wellness
BEST_TIME: September to June (best: Feb-May, Sep-Nov)
AVOID: July-August (monsoon floods, rafting suspended)
ALTITUDE: 372 m
HIGHLIGHTS:
- Ganga Aarti at Triveni Ghat: Daily at sunset — mesmerizing experience
- Laxman Jhula & Ram Jhula: Iconic suspension bridges over Ganga
- White Water Rafting: 16km (Shivpuri to Rishikesh) to 36km (Marine Drive), Grade 3-4
- Bungee Jumping: Jumpin Heights (83m), India's highest fixed platform bungee
- Beatles Ashram (Chaurasi Kutia): Where Beatles meditated in 1968, now ruins art gallery
- Neelkanth Mahadev Temple: 15km from Rishikesh, Lord Shiva temple in forest
- Rajaji National Park: Wildlife safari — elephants, tigers, leopards
- Flying Fox: 1.8km zipline over Ganga
- Camping on Ganga banks: Popular at Shivpuri (16km upstream)
- Yoga & Meditation centers: 200+ ashrams — Parmarth Niketan, Sivananda Ashram, Ananda Spa
BUDGET_RANGE:
- Budget: ₹1,500-3,000/day (ashram stay, street food, shared auto)
- Mid-range: ₹3,500-7,000/day (hotel, restaurant, adventure activities)
- Luxury: ₹10,000-25,000/day (Ananda Spa Resort, Aloha on the Ganges)
IDEAL_FOR: Spiritual seekers, Adventure lovers, Solo travelers, Yoga practitioners, Couples
DISTANCE_FROM_DELHI: 250 km (5-6 hrs by road)
NEAREST_AIRPORT: Jolly Grant Airport (Dehradun), 35 km
FAMOUS_ACTIVITIES: Rafting (₹600-1,500), Bungee (₹3,500), Camping (₹800-2,500/night with meals)
CONNECTIVITY: Good Jio/Airtel connectivity

DESTINATION: Jaipur
STATE: Rajasthan
TYPE: Heritage, Culture, Shopping, Royal experience
BEST_TIME: October to March
AVOID: April to June (extreme heat 42-48°C)
HIGHLIGHTS:
- Amber Fort (Amer Fort): 16th century hilltop fort, elephant ride up (₹900), stunning mirror work
- Hawa Mahal: 5-story palace of winds, 953 windows, iconic photo stop
- City Palace: Royal residence, museum, Diwan-i-Khas with world's largest silver vessels
- Jantar Mantar: UNESCO World Heritage, 18th century astronomical instruments
- Nahargarh Fort: Sunset point, overlooks entire pink city
- Jaigarh Fort: Asia's largest cannon 'Jaivana', underground treasury tunnels
- Johri Bazaar: Gems and jewelry shopping — Jaipur is world's largest gemstone trading hub
- Bapu Bazaar: Textiles, mojris (shoes), handicrafts, block prints
- Birla Mandir: White marble temple, evening prayers
- Chokhi Dhani: Ethnic Rajasthani village resort, folk performances, traditional dinner
BUDGET_RANGE:
- Budget: ₹2,000-4,000/day (hostel, local dhabas, auto-rickshaw)
- Mid-range: ₹5,000-9,000/day (hotel, restaurant, taxi day tours)
- Luxury: ₹15,000-50,000/day (palace hotels like Rambagh, Raj Palace)
IDEAL_FOR: History lovers, Couples, Families, Photographers, Shopping enthusiasts
DISTANCE_FROM_DELHI: 270 km (5 hrs by car, 4.5 hrs by train)
NEAREST_AIRPORT: Jaipur International Airport (JAI)
CONNECTIVITY: Excellent Jio/Airtel/Vi coverage
DAY_TRIPS: Ajmer-Pushkar (135km), Ranthambore Tiger Reserve (180km), Abhaneri Step Well (95km)

DESTINATION: Jodhpur
STATE: Rajasthan
TYPE: Heritage, Culture, Blue City, Desert
BEST_TIME: October to March
HIGHLIGHTS:
- Mehrangarh Fort: Most majestic fort in India, 400ft above city, 7 gates, museum
- Jaswant Thada: White marble cenotaph, called Taj Mahal of Marwar
- Umaid Bhawan Palace: Part palace hotel, part museum, Art Deco architecture
- Ghanta Ghar (Clock Tower): Heart of old city bazaar
- Blue City Walk: Navigate maze of indigo-painted houses in old city
- Mandore Gardens: Ancient crematorium of Marwar rulers, has sculptures of Hindu deities
- Balsamand Lake: 12th century artificial lake, heritage hotel
BUDGET_RANGE:
- Budget: ₹1,800-3,500/day
- Mid-range: ₹4,000-8,000/day
- Luxury: ₹20,000-80,000/day (Umaid Bhawan Palace — one of world's best hotels)
IDEAL_FOR: History lovers, Photography, Heritage stays, Couples

DESTINATION: Jaisalmer
STATE: Rajasthan
TYPE: Desert, Heritage, Camel Safari, Camping
BEST_TIME: October to March (avoid summer — 45°C+)
HIGHLIGHTS:
- Jaisalmer Fort: Living fort, 99 bastions, 250,000 people still live inside
- Sam Sand Dunes: 42km from city, best for sunset camel safari, overnight desert camping
- Khuri Sand Dunes: Less touristy than Sam, more authentic experience
- Patwon Ki Haveli: Most elaborate haveli in Jaisalmer, 5-story intricate carvings
- Gadisar Lake: Artificial lake, boat rides, migratory birds, temples
- Kuldhara Ghost Village: Abandoned village, cursed legend, 200 years empty
BUDGET_RANGE:
- Budget: ₹2,000-4,000/day
- Mid-range: ₹4,500-8,000/day
- Luxury: ₹10,000-25,000/day (luxury desert camps)
FAMOUS_ACTIVITIES: Camel safari (₹500-2,000), Desert camping with cultural show (₹2,500-8,000/night)

DESTINATION: Varanasi
STATE: Uttar Pradesh
TYPE: Spiritual, Cultural, Historical
BEST_TIME: October to March
HIGHLIGHTS:
- Dashashwamedh Ghat: Most famous ghat, spectacular Ganga Aarti every evening
- Manikarnika Ghat: The burning ghat, cremation site, deeply spiritual
- Assi Ghat: Popular with students, morning yoga, evening music
- Kashi Vishwanath Temple: One of 12 Jyotirlingas, rebuilt by Ahilya Bai Holkar
- Sarnath: 10km from Varanasi, where Buddha gave first sermon
- Boat ride at sunrise on Ganga: Most iconic Varanasi experience
- Banaras Hindu University: One of Asia's largest universities, Bharat Kala Bhavan museum
BUDGET_RANGE:
- Budget: ₹1,500-3,000/day
- Mid-range: ₹3,500-7,000/day
- Luxury: ₹10,000-20,000/day (Taj Nadesar Palace, BrijRama Palace)

DESTINATION: Goa
STATE: Goa
TYPE: Beach, Nightlife, Heritage, Culture, Water Sports
BEST_TIME: November to February (peak), October and March (shoulder — fewer crowds)
AVOID: June to September (monsoon, most beach shacks closed)
HIGHLIGHTS:
- North Goa Beaches: Baga (nightlife), Calangute (crowded, shopping), Anjuna (flea market, cliffs), Vagator (scenic), Arambol (hippie, music)
- South Goa Beaches: Palolem (crescent shaped, calm), Agonda (quiet, turtles), Benaulim, Colva
- Old Goa (Velha Goa): UNESCO Heritage — Basilica of Bom Jesus (St. Francis Xavier relics), Se Cathedral, Church of St. Francis of Assisi
- Dudhsagar Falls: 310m waterfall on Goa-Karnataka border, jeep safari (₹2,500/jeep)
- Spice Plantations: Sahakari Spice Farm, Savoi Plantation — tour + lunch (₹500-800)
- Aguada Fort: 17th century Portuguese fort, lighthouse
- Chapora Fort: Famous from Dil Chahta Hai movie, sunset views
- Saturday Night Market (Arpora): Flea market with live music, food, handicrafts
- Anjuna Flea Market: Every Wednesday, hippie culture, antiques, clothes
- Nightlife: Tito's Lane Baga, Curlies Anjuna, Hilltop Vagator, Sinq Calangute
WATER_SPORTS: Parasailing (₹600-1,000), Jet ski (₹600-1,000/15min), Banana boat (₹200/person), Scuba diving (₹3,500-5,000), Snorkeling at Grande Island (₹1,500)
BUDGET_RANGE:
- Budget: ₹2,000-4,000/day (hostel near beach, beach shack meals, rented scooter)
- Mid-range: ₹5,000-9,000/day (3-4 star hotel, restaurant meals, activities)
- Luxury: ₹12,000-35,000/day (Taj Exotica, W Goa, Alila Diwa)
IDEAL_FOR: Couples, Groups, Solo travelers, Party lovers, Water sports, Families
NEAREST_AIRPORTS: Dabolim/Mopa (Goa International)
LOCAL_TRANSPORT: Scooter rental ₹300-500/day (recommended), Goa Miles app taxis

DESTINATION: Munnar
STATE: Kerala
TYPE: Hill Station, Tea Gardens, Nature, Honeymoon
BEST_TIME: September to May (best: Oct-Feb)
ALTITUDE: 1,600 m
HIGHLIGHTS:
- Tea Gardens: KDHP Tea Museum, rolling tea estates — most photogenic in India
- Eravikulam National Park: Home to Nilgiri Tahr (endangered), Neelakurinji flowers (bloom once in 12 years, next 2030)
- Mattupetty Dam: Lake, boating, Indo-Swiss Livestock Project dairy farm
- Top Station: Highest point in Munnar (1,700m), view into Tamil Nadu
- Attukal Waterfalls: 8km from Munnar, trek through tea estates
- Lockhart Gap: Viewpoint, misty valleys
- Rajamala: Wildlife, trekking, Nilgiri Tahr spotting
BUDGET_RANGE:
- Budget: ₹2,000-4,000/day
- Mid-range: ₹5,000-10,000/day
- Luxury: ₹12,000-25,000/day (tea estate bungalow stays)
IDEAL_FOR: Honeymoon, Couples, Nature lovers, Families

DESTINATION: Alleppey (Alappuzha)
STATE: Kerala
TYPE: Backwaters, Houseboat, Nature
BEST_TIME: September to March
HIGHLIGHTS:
- Alleppey Backwaters: 900km network of canals, lagoons, lakes
- Houseboat (Kettuvallam): Overnight stay on traditional rice barge — most iconic Kerala experience
- Nehru Trophy Boat Race: Held 2nd Saturday of August — massive snake boat race
- Alleppey Beach: Lighthouse, pier, beach walks
- Kumarakom Bird Sanctuary: 14 acres, migratory birds Nov-Feb
- Marari Beach: 12km from Alleppey, pristine, less crowded, fishing village feel
- Kuttanad: Below sea-level paddy fields, called Rice Bowl of Kerala
BUDGET_RANGE:
- Budget: ₹2,000-3,500/day (budget houseboat ₹5,000-8,000/night for 2)
- Mid-range: ₹5,000-10,000/day (premium houseboat ₹10,000-18,000/night)
- Luxury: ₹15,000-35,000/day (Taj Kumarakom, luxury houseboat)
HOUSEBOAT_TIP: Book directly with operators at boat jetty for 20-30% discount vs. online

DESTINATION: Coorg (Kodagu)

- Mid-range: ₹5,000-9,000/day (plantation homestay)
- Luxury: ₹12,000-30,000/day (Orange County, Taj Madikeri)

DESTINATION: Ooty (Udhagamandalam)
STATE: Tamil Nadu
TYPE: Hill Station, Nilgiri Hills, Nature

- Luxury: ₹10,000-20,000/day (Savoy Hotel — heritage property)

DESTINATION: Andaman Islands
STATE: Andaman and Nicobar Islands (Union Territory)
TYPE: Beaches, Scuba Diving, Snorkeling, Nature, History
BEST_TIME: November to May
AVOID: June to September (heavy monsoon, ferries cancelled)
HIGHLIGHTS:
- Radhanagar Beach (Havelock): Asia's best beach (Time magazine), pristine white sand, turquoise water
- Cellular Jail (Port Blair): Colonial prison, National Memorial, Light & Sound show evenings
- Ross Island: Former British capital, ruins overtaken by deer and peacocks
- Havelock Island (Swaraj Dweep): Radhanagar Beach, Elephant Beach, scuba diving
- Neil Island (Shaheed Dweep): Natural Bridge, Bharatpur Beach, coral reefs
- North Bay Island: Glass bottom boat, snorkeling, sea walk
- Baratang Island: Limestone caves, mangrove creeks, mud volcanoes
- Mahatma Gandhi Marine National Park: Wandoor Beach, coral reefs, tropical fish
DIVING_SPOTS: Lighthouse (beginner), Minerva Ledge, Mac Point, Aquarium (intermediate), Barracuda City (advanced)
BUDGET_RANGE:
- Budget: ₹3,000-5,500/day (budget hotel Port Blair, ferry travel)
- Mid-range: ₹6,000-12,000/day (mid-range resort Havelock, activities)
- Luxury: ₹15,000-40,000/day (Taj Exotica, Jalakara, Barefoot at Havelock)
NEAREST_AIRPORT: Veer Savarkar International Airport (IXZ), Port Blair
IDEAL_FOR: Couples, Honeymooners, Divers, Beach lovers, Photographers
FERRY_INFO: Makruzz and Nautika ferries — Port Blair to Havelock (90 min, ₹1,000-1,800). Book in advance.
PERMIT: Restricted Area Permit (RAP) — issued free on arrival at Port Blair airport for Indian nationals

DESTINATION: Lakshadweep
STATE: Lakshadweep (Union Territory)
TYPE: Coral Islands, Diving, Snorkeling, Pristine Beaches
BEST_TIME: October to May
HIGHLIGHTS:
- Agatti Island: Entry point, lagoon, world-class snorkeling
- Bangaram Island: Uninhabited, pristine, exclusive resort
- Kavaratti: Capital island, mosques, marine aquarium
- Minicoy Island: Southernmost, lighthouse, Mahl culture distinct from rest
- Coral reefs: Among best preserved in world, 4,000+ marine species
PERMIT: Entry Permit required — only through registered tour operators (foreigners need special permit)
BUDGET_RANGE:
- Budget: ₹8,000-15,000/day (SPORTS packages from Kerala government)
- Luxury: ₹20,000-60,000/day (Bangaram Island Resort, Agatti Island Beach Resort)

DESTINATION: Meghalaya (Shillong + Cherrapunji + Dawki)
STATE: Meghalaya
TYPE: Nature, Waterfalls, Living Root Bridges, Adventure
BEST_TIME: October to May (waterfalls best Oct-Dec after monsoon)
HIGHLIGHTS:
- Living Root Bridges: Double Decker Root Bridge at Nongriat (3km trek) — UNESCO nomination pending
- Dawki (Umngot River): Crystal clear river, boating — boat appears to float on air
- Nohkalikai Falls: India's tallest plunge waterfall (340m) near Cherrapunji
- Mawsynram: Wettest place on earth
- Elephant Falls: Shillong, 3-tiered waterfall
- Mawlynnong: Asia's Cleanest Village, living root bridge, sky walk
- Shnongpdeng: Camping on Umngot river banks, kayaking, ziplining
- Ward's Lake: Shillong, colonial-era boating lake
- Don Bosco Museum: Best cultural museum of Northeast India
BUDGET_RANGE:
- Budget: ₹2,000-4,000/day
- Mid-range: ₹4,500-8,000/day
- Luxury: ₹10,000-20,000/day
IDEAL_FOR: Nature lovers, Photographers, Adventure seekers, Solo travelers
NEAREST_AIRPORT: LGBI Airport Guwahati (Assam), 100km from Shillong

DESTINATION: Sikkim (Gangtok + Lachung + Pelling)
STATE: Sikkim
TYPE: Himalayan, Spiritual, Nature, Adventure
BEST_TIME: March to May (flowers), October to December (clear skies, Kanchenjunga views)
HIGHLIGHTS:
- Tsomgo (Changu) Lake: Sacred lake, 38km from Gangtok, Yak rides
- Nathula Pass (4,310m): India-China border, permits required
- Rumtek Monastery: Largest monastery in Sikkim, seat of Kagyu sect
- Gurudongmar Lake (5,183m): One of world's highest lakes, North Sikkim
- Yumthang Valley: Valley of Flowers, rhododendrons March-May
- Zero Point: 4,428m, snow even in summer
- Pelling: Kanchenjunga views, Rabdentse Ruins, Pemayangtse Monastery
- Goecha La Trek: 16-day trek to base of Kanchenjunga
PERMITS: Inner Line Permit for North Sikkim (Lachung, Gurudongmar) and Nathula Pass
BUDGET_RANGE:
- Budget: ₹2,500-4,500/day
- Mid-range: ₹5,000-9,000/day
- Luxury: ₹12,000-25,000/day (Mayfair Gangtok, Elgin Mount Pandim)
