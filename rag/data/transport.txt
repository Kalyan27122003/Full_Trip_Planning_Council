# FROM HYDERABAD (RGIA) — ALL ROUTES
# ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

ROUTE: Hyderabad to Goa
FLIGHT:
- Airlines: IndiGo (cheapest), Air India, SpiceJet, Vistara
- Duration: 1 hr 20 min (direct)
- Avg Price: ₹2,500-7,000 one way (book 30+ days ahead for deals)
- Airports: RGIA Hyderabad (HYD) → Dabolim (GOI) or Mopa (GOX — newer, North Goa)
- Frequency: 5-8 flights daily
- Best Booking: Skyscanner, Google Flights, MakeMyTrip
- TIP: Mopa airport better if staying North Goa; Dabolim better for South Goa
TRAIN:
- Konark Express (11302): Departs SECUNDERABAD 11:35, arrives MADGAON (Goa) 09:10 next day — 21.5 hrs, overnight
- Goa Express (12779): Departs HAZUR SAHIB NANDED, passes Hyderabad, arrives Vasco — check timetable
- Classes: 2AC (₹1,000-1,600), 3AC (₹700-1,100), Sleeper (₹350-550)
- Booking: IRCTC.co.in (book 120 days in advance)
- TIP: Book 2AC for comfortable overnight journey
BUS:
- Operators: VRL Travels (premium), Paulo Travels, Shreenath Travel, SRS Travels
- Bus Types: Volvo AC Sleeper (recommended), Semi-sleeper, Non-AC
- Duration: 12-14 hours (overnight)
- Price: ₹800-2,000 (Volvo AC Sleeper)
- Pickup Points: Uppal, Majestic (Hyderabad), JBS
- Booking: redBus.in, AbhiBus.com
- TIP: Paulo Travels is legendary for Goa buses — book early in peak season
LOCAL_TRANSPORT_GOA:
- Scooter rental: ₹300-500/day (best way to explore, Activa or Scooty) — carry license
- Bike rental: ₹800-1,500/day (Royal Enfield for longer trips)
- Goa Miles app: Metered taxis (fairer pricing than tourist taxis)
- Tourist taxi: ₹2,000-3,500/day with driver (no meter — negotiate before)
- Shared buses: ₹10-60 per trip (Kadamba Transport)
- Auto-rickshaw: Only in Panaji, not common at beaches

ROUTE: Hyderabad to Manali
FLIGHT:
- Via Delhi: IndiGo/Air India HYD→DEL (2 hrs, ₹3,000-7,000) + Kullu-Bhuntar flight (1 hr, ₹3,000-6,000)
- Delhi to Bhuntar (KUU): Air India, SpiceJet — 1 hr
- From Bhuntar to Manali: Taxi 2.5 hrs (₹1,500-2,000) or bus ₹150
- HRTC Volvo bus Delhi→Manali: Departs 17:00, arrives 07:00 (₹700-1,400)
- TIP: For pure comfort, fly to Bhuntar. For adventure, overnight Volvo from Delhi.
TRAIN:
- Hyderabad to Chandigarh: Godavari Express or Nanded Express via Delhi (18-22 hrs)
- Chandigarh to Manali: HRTC bus (7-8 hrs, ₹500-900)
- OR: Hyderabad to Delhi (Rajdhani Express, 26 hrs), then overnight Volvo to Manali
ROAD_FROM_DELHI:
- Delhi to Manali via Kullu: 570km, 12-14 hrs by car
- HRTC Volvo: Departs ISBT Kashmiri Gate Delhi 17:00 daily ₹700-1,400
- Private car hire: ₹12,000-18,000 (SUV Delhi→Manali)
LOCAL_TRANSPORT_MANALI:
- Local taxis: Fixed rates — Manali to Rohtang ₹1,800-2,200 (mandatory permit vehicle), Solang Valley ₹600-800
- Shared jeeps: To Old Manali ₹20, to Vashisht ₹15
- Bike rental: ₹600-1,000/day (Bullet/Enfield), ₹300-500/day (scooter)
- Walking: Old Manali to Mall Road is 3km, pleasant walk

ROUTE: Hyderabad to Leh-Ladakh
FLIGHT:
- Via Delhi ONLY recommended: HYD→DEL (2 hrs) + DEL→LEH (1.5 hrs)
- Airlines: Air India, IndiGo, SpiceJet (Leh route)
- Hyderabad to Delhi: ₹3,000-7,000 | Delhi to Leh: ₹4,000-10,000
- CRITICAL TIP: Leh flights often get cancelled due to weather. Have buffer days in Delhi.
- Morning flights to Leh preferred (clearer weather before noon)
ROAD_ALTERNATIVE (Adventure seekers):
- Manali-Leh Highway: 480km, 2 days, open June-October only
- Day 1: Manali → Sarchu (230km, 8-10 hrs, altitude: 4,290m)
- Day 2: Sarchu → Leh (250km, 8-10 hrs)
- Srinagar-Leh Highway: 434km, 2 days, open May-November
- Shared jeep Manali→Leh: ₹1,200-2,000/person (overcrowded)
- HRTC bus Manali→Leh: ₹700-1,000 (2-day journey with night halt)
LOCAL_TRANSPORT_LADAKH:
- Leh airport to city: Pre-paid taxi ₹300-500 (3km)
- Hired taxis for sightseeing: Fixed rates (Leh taxi union)
  * Pangong Lake day trip: ₹3,500-4,500
  * Nubra Valley 2 days: ₹6,000-8,000
  * Tso Moriri 2 days: ₹7,000-9,000
- Bike rental (Leh): Royal Enfield ₹1,200-2,000/day, Hero ₹800-1,200/day
- Shared jeep to Pangong: ₹400-600 per seat (from Leh Jeep Stand)
- BSNL SIM essential — buy at Leh airport

ROUTE: Hyderabad to Kerala (Kochi / Munnar / Alleppey)
FLIGHT:
- HYD→COK (Kochi): IndiGo, Air India, SpiceJet — 1 hr 20 min direct
- Price: ₹2,500-6,500 one way
- Frequency: 8-10 flights daily
- TIP: Kochi (Cochin) airport is best base for all of Kerala
TRAIN:
- Hyderabad to Ernakulam (Kochi): Sabari Express, Mumbai-Alappuzha Express, Netravati Express
- Duration: 14-18 hrs
- Price: Sleeper ₹450-650, 3AC ₹1,000-1,400, 2AC ₹1,400-1,900
- TIP: Duronto Express is fastest (14 hrs) but fewer stops
BUS:
- KSRTC Kerala and TSRTC Telangana operate overnight buses
- Duration: 14-16 hrs
- Volvo AC Sleeper: ₹1,000-1,800
LOCAL_TRANSPORT_KERALA:
- Kochi: Metro rail (₹10-60), auto-rickshaw (₹30-200), app-based taxis
- Munnar: Hired jeep with driver ₹2,500-3,500/day (best for tea garden exploration)
- Alleppey: Auto-rickshaw ₹50-200, cycle rental ₹100-200/day, ferry ₹5-20 per trip
- Backwater ferry (public): Alleppey to Kottayam ₹20-30 — scenic 2.5 hr journey vs ₹1,200 taxi

ROUTE: Hyderabad to Andaman Islands
FLIGHT:
- Via Kolkata or Chennai ONLY (no direct from Hyderabad)
- HYD→CCU (Kolkata) + CCU→IXZ (Port Blair): IndiGo, Air India — total 4-5 hrs
- Or HYD→MAA (Chennai) + MAA→IXZ: 1 hr 20 min + 2 hrs = 3.5 hrs total travel
- Price: HYD→Port Blair total ₹7,000-18,000 round trip
- TIP: Check seasonal sales on IndiGo — Andaman tickets under ₹4,000 one way sometimes available
SHIP_ALTERNATIVE:
- Ship from Chennai/Kolkata to Port Blair: 60-70 hours (not recommended for tourists)
- Phoenix Bay Shipping Corporation — ₹1,500-5,000 depending on class
ANDAMAN_INTER_ISLAND:
- Port Blair to Havelock: Makruzz (premium, 90 min, ₹1,200-1,800) or Government ferry (2.5 hrs, ₹300)
- Port Blair to Neil Island: Makruzz (80 min, ₹1,100-1,600) or Government ferry (2 hrs, ₹250)
- Havelock to Neil: Makruzz (45 min, ₹900-1,200)
- TIP: Book Makruzz tickets online in advance — makruzz.com — they get sold out quickly in peak season
LOCAL_TRANSPORT_ANDAMAN:
- Port Blair: Auto-rickshaw (₹50-150), taxi (₹200-500), hired bike (₹400-600/day)
- Havelock: Hired scooter (₹400-600/day) — essential, as island is spread out
- Neil Island: Bicycle (₹100-150/day) — small island, easy cycling

# ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
# BOOKING PLATFORMS & TIPS
# ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

FLIGHT_BOOKING:
- Skyscanner.com: Best for comparing across airlines, set fare alerts
- Google Flights: Excellent calendar view to find cheapest dates
- MakeMyTrip.com: Good for Indian domestic flights + hotel combos
- EaseMyTrip.com: Often cheaper service fees than MakeMyTrip
- Book directly on airline website for best cancellation policies
- Cheap flight days: Tuesday, Wednesday flights often 10-15% cheaper
- Best booking window: 3-6 weeks in advance for domestic India flights

TRAIN_BOOKING:
- IRCTC.co.in (official): Book 120 days in advance (60 days for some trains)
- Tatkal quota: Opens 1 day before travel, 30% premium — emergency option
- Current Booking: Some seats released 4 hrs before departure (unreserved)
- Classes explained:
  * 1AC: Most expensive, private cabin, 4 berths per compartment
  * 2AC: Comfortable, 8 berths per compartment, bedding included, ₹1,000-2,500
  * 3AC: Most popular among travelers, 8 berths, good value, ₹600-1,500
  * Sleeper (SL): Budget, no AC, 8 berths, ₹200-600
  * Shatabdi/Duronto/Rajdhani: Premium express trains, faster schedules

BUS_BOOKING:
- redBus.in: Largest bus booking platform in India
- AbhiBus.com: Good for comparing operators
- State transport (KSRTC, HRTC, RSRTC): Official state bus bookings
- Popular premium operators: VRL Travels, Paulo Travels, SRS Travels, Kallada Travels

APP_TAXIS:
- OLA: Available in most Indian cities (often cheaper than Uber)
- Uber: Available in major cities, international payment accepted
- Rapido: Bike taxis — cheap for short distances (₹30-100)
- InDrive: Negotiate price directly with driver
